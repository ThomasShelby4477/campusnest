# About CampusNest

**CampusNest** was born out of a simple necessity: the struggle to find safe, affordable, and compatible student housing near the National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU).

## The Problem
Every semester, hundreds of students face the daunting task of finding reliable accommodation and trustworthy roommates. Scams, exorbitant broker fees, and mismatched living situations are far too common.

## Our Mission
Our mission is to create a secure, verified, and student-first housing ecosystem. By exclusively serving the NFSU community and verifying every user, we ensure that you can find your next home—and your next roommate—with peace of mind.

## The Team
We are a small team of engineers and designers dedicated to solving real-world problems for students. CampusNest is built with modern technology, focusing on privacy, speed, and beautiful design.
