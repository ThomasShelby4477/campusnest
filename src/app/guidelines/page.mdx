# Community Guidelines

CampusNest is built exclusively for the NFSU community. To ensure a safe and respectful environment, all users must adhere to the following guidelines.

## 1. Zero Tolerance Policy
We have a zero tolerance policy for the following behaviors:
- Harassment or bullying of any kind.
- Hate speech or abusive language.
- Posting fake, scam, or misleading listings.
- Discrimination based on caste, religion, gender, or region.

## 2. Platform Usage
- **No Brokers Allowed:** This platform is designed for direct student-to-student and student-to-landlord interactions. Broker commissions are strictly prohibited.
- **Student-Only Space:** Student profiles are restricted to verified NFSU students. Non-students posing as students will be reported and banned.

## 3. Reporting & Consequences
Every user profile and listing has a "Report" button. If you encounter violations of these guidelines, please report them immediately.
- **Warning:** Minor infractions may result in an official warning.
- **Suspension:** Repeated or serious infractions will lead to temporary account suspension.
- **Permanent Ban:** Severe violations (e.g., scams, harassment) will result in an immediate and permanent ban.
