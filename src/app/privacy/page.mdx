# Privacy Policy

CampusNest is committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023.

## 1. Data Collected
We collect the following personal data to provide our services:
- **Profile Information:** Name, email, phone number, gender, year, and branch.
- **Verification Documents:** Student ID cards and optional selfies (stored securely in a private bucket).
- **Usage Data:** Device information, location (for listings), and system usage logs.

## 2. Purpose of Collection
Your data is exclusively used for:
- Verifying your identity as a valid NFSU student or property owner.
- Delivering platform services such as property listings and roommate matching.
- Ensuring platform safety and dispute resolution.

## 3. Data Sharing & Retention
- **No Third-Party Sales:** We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.
- **Limited Access:** Data is shared with administrators solely for verification and moderation purposes.
- **Retention:** Data is retained for the lifetime of your active account plus 3 years post-deletion for legal compliance.

## 4. Your Rights
Under the DPDP Act, you have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request corrections to inaccurate information.
- Request the deletion of your account and personal data (which will be processed within 30 days).
- File grievances regarding data handling.

## 5. Contact
For any privacy-related queries or data deletion requests, please contact us at **privacy@campusnest.in**.
